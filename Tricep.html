..TRICEPS EXERCISES..

TRICEPS DIP Dips are a proven mass-builder on their own.Lots of people can't do enough reps.

TIP: Dips hit both the chest and triceps, but to favor the tris, keep your body vertical,keep those elbows tight to your sides.

CABLE PUllDOWN The Cable Extension is a great tool for beginners before moving on to more demanding exercises.

TIP: A strict posture is key to isolating the triceps effectively. Position yourself with a balanced stance, soft knees and retracted shoulders; keeping your elbows pinned throughout the movement.

SKULL CRUSHER If you're chasing big triceps, the skull crusher exercise should be near the top of your list.

TIP:Don't drop the weight; else you'll find out why this exercise is called the "skull crusher".

TRICEPS DIP This compound exer- cise makes it easy to pick and change weights for different rep ranges.

TIP:Many trainees mess up here by failing to go to full triceps extension and stopping short of fully bending their elbows. Your triceps are elbow extenders, so perform the full range of motion.

Tate Press It's usually done on a bench at incline,this move isolates the triceps without recru- iting shoulders,back.

TIP:Lie on back on mat with knees bent. Hold a dumbbell in each hand facing forward and extend them shoulder-width apart with elbows pointing out.
